FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before using CareConnect.

Is my identity really anonymous?

Yes. During registration your identity is verified by the system, but your identity remains hidden whenever you post. Only anonymous content is visible to others.

Who can reply to my posts?

Only verified psychologists approved by the administrator can respond to your anonymous posts.

Can I book an appointment?

Yes. Users can request appointments with verified psychologists directly through CareConnect.

Is CareConnect free to use?

Yes. Registration, anonymous posting and receiving guidance are free for users.

How are psychologists verified?

Each psychologist submits professional documents which are manually verified by the administrator.

Can I delete my anonymous posts?

Yes. You can edit or permanently delete your own posts whenever you want.

CareConnect is a secure and anonymous mental health support platform connecting individuals with verified psychologists in a safe, confidential, and supportive environment.
