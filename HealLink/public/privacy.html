Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

At CareConnect, protecting your privacy is our highest priority. We are committed to maintaining a secure, confidential, and anonymous environment where users can seek professional mental health support with confidence.

Last Updated: July 2026

Introduction

CareConnect is an anonymous mental health support platform designed to connect individuals with verified psychologists. We understand the importance of protecting personal information, especially when dealing with mental health. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information while using our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Full Name during registration
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Identity verification documents (only for verification purposes)
  • Anonymous mental health posts
  • Appointment information
  • Login activity and session information

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To verify your identity.
  • To schedule counseling appointments.
  • To improve user experience.
  • To provide secure communication with psychologists.
  • To maintain platform security.

Anonymous Posting

Your identity is never displayed when creating anonymous posts. Other users and psychologists can only view the anonymous content of your post. Personal information remains hidden and protected within the system.

Data Security

  • Passwords are securely encrypted.
  • Role-based authentication is implemented.
  • Only authorized administrators can access sensitive information.
  • Secure sessions protect user accounts.
  • Regular backups are maintained.

Psychologist Verification

Every psychologist registered on CareConnect must submit professional verification documents. Only verified psychologists are allowed to provide counseling and respond to anonymous posts.

Confidentiality

All conversations between users and psychologists remain confidential. CareConnect does not publicly disclose counseling records or personal discussions.

Your Rights

  • Update your profile.
  • Delete your anonymous posts.
  • Request account deletion.
  • Change your password anytime.
  • Control your appointment information.

Policy Updates

CareConnect may update this Privacy Policy whenever new features or legal requirements are introduced. Users will be notified about significant changes through the platform.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the CareConnect support team through the Contact page or by emailing our official support address.

CareConnect is a secure and anonymous mental health support platform connecting individuals with verified psychologists in a safe, confidential, and supportive environment.
